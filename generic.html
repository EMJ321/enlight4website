nalu

A decorative floor lamp for luxurious hotel lobbies, nalu serves to calm visitors who come into the lobby with its small flickers of light, bringing refreshing peace and wonder to whoever sets their eyes upon it.

Inspired by the curvatures of ocean waves and the fluidity of water, I focused on designing an elegant organic form that expresses movement whether it be through the lighting or through the physical form itself.
